Here is my story

My name is Camryn Katherine DeVos. I am currently attending the University of California Riverside as a third-year student. My first year of college I attended California State University of San Marcos, however, I saw a better fit for myself in attending University of California Riverside. Going into college I had always intended to graduate with a degree in Business, thus, when I started attending the University of California Riverside I started my path in majoring as a Business Economics major. Unfortunately, up until recently, I saw a better fit for myself if I majored in Political Science. After reviewing with my academic advisor, I decided to make the switch to this new major. As terrifying as this decision was, I made the leap and I am currently a Political Science major with a focus in International Affairs. Political Science aims to provide an understanding of basic political processes and institutions as these operate in different national and cultural contexts. And being a focus in International Affairs refer to events and activities that involve the governments, politics, economies, etc. of different countries. I am focused on graduating at this time and I should receive my Bachelor's degree by the end of next spring of 2026.
